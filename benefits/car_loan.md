# Car Loan

We understand that reliable personal transport is important for our team, especially when working across multiple sites. On completion of your probation period, we offer an interest-free car loan of up to one month's net salary to help you purchase or maintain a vehicle.

## How it works

- The loan is available to all permanent employees who have successfully completed their probation period.
- The maximum loan amount is equivalent to one month's net salary.
- The loan is repaid automatically from your monthly net salary in equal instalments over an agreed repayment period (typically 12 months).
- No interest is charged on the loan.

## Applying

To apply, submit a request to the People team at [people@afiax.africa](mailto:people@afiax.africa) with the purpose and amount needed. You will receive a response within five business days.

## Leaving the company

If you leave Afiax before the loan is fully repaid, the outstanding balance will be deducted from your final salary payment.
