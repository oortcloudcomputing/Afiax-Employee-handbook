# Health Insurance

Afiax provides comprehensive health insurance cover for all permanent employees, starting from your first day of employment.

## What is covered

Our health insurance plan covers:

- **Inpatient care** – hospitalisation, surgery, and specialist consultations
- **Outpatient care** – doctor visits, diagnostics, and pharmacy
- **Maternity care** – antenatal, delivery, and postnatal cover
- **Dental and optical** – routine dental checks and optical care
- **Emergency care** – 24/7 emergency cover including ambulance

## Dependents

You may extend cover to your spouse and up to four dependent children at a subsidised rate. Contact the People team to add dependents to your policy.

## Provider and panel hospitals

Our cover is provided through a leading Kenyan medical insurer with a wide panel of hospitals and clinics across Kenya and East Africa. Full details of the panel and how to access care will be shared with you during onboarding.

## How to access your cover

You will receive your insurance card and member number during your first week. Present your card at any panel facility to access cashless treatment.

For urgent queries or pre-authorisation for planned procedures, contact the insurer's 24-hour helpline — details are on the back of your insurance card.
