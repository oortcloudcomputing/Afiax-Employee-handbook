# House Buying Plan

We believe that financial stability and home ownership are important to the long-term wellbeing of our team. To support this, Afiax offers a House Buying Plan to help employees take the step toward owning their first home.

## Eligibility

The House Buying Plan is available to permanent employees who have completed at least two years of continuous service with Afiax.

## What we offer

- An interest-free salary advance of up to three months' net salary to contribute toward a house deposit or purchase costs.
- Repayment is spread over 24 months via automatic salary deductions.
- Employees may only access this benefit once.

## Process

1. Speak with your Line Manager about your plans.
2. Submit a written request to [people@afiax.africa](mailto:people@afiax.africa) including a brief description of the intended purchase.
3. The People team will review your request and respond within ten business days.
4. Once approved, funds are disbursed to you directly and a repayment schedule is agreed.

## Leaving the company

If you leave Afiax before the advance is fully repaid, the outstanding balance will be deducted from your final salary payment. If the final salary is insufficient to cover the balance, alternative arrangements will be agreed in writing.

## Questions

Contact the People team at [people@afiax.africa](mailto:people@afiax.africa) with any questions about this benefit.
