# About Afiax Connected Healthcare

As a **purpose-driven organisation**, we believe the **outcomes** we create matter more than the technology we deliver. Every line of code we write, every system we build, exists to move healthcare forward — for patients, clinicians, and communities across Africa.

## Our purpose – why we do what we do

Africa carries a disproportionate share of the world's disease burden while being the most underserved by connected, interoperable health systems. Fragmented records, duplicated effort, and missing data cost lives.

We exist to change that.

Afiax builds open, standards-based digital health infrastructure that lets health systems talk to each other, clinicians make better decisions, and patients receive care that follows them wherever they go — across providers, counties, and borders.

## Our vision – what we want the future to look like

A pan-African health system where every patient has a portable, FHIR-native health record; every clinician has the information they need at the point of care; and every health authority can see, plan, and respond to the population's needs in real time.

We start in Kenya — integrating with the Digital Health Authority's national HIE (AfyaLink) and the Social Health Authority (SHA) claims platform — and we build every feature as a reusable **country pack** that can be deployed across Africa without starting from scratch.

## Our missions – how we will get there

- **Interoperate** – Build on FHIR R4 and open standards so Afiax systems connect seamlessly with national health information exchanges, insurance platforms, and global health tools
- **Localise** – Design country packs that reflect real workflows, real clinical terminology, and real infrastructure constraints in each African market
- **Capacitate** – Grow local engineering and health informatics talent so African health systems are owned and operated by Africans
- **Accelerate** – Help hospitals, clinics, and health authorities go from paper to digital, and from digital silos to connected ecosystems, faster than they could alone
- **Sustain** – Build systems that outlast any single engagement, with the open-source foundations, documentation, and training to prove it

## Our values – who we are and how we operate

**Patient first** — Every feature request, every architectural decision, every trade-off is evaluated against one question: does this improve care for the patient? Technology is the means; better health outcomes are the end.

**Open by default** — We build on open standards (FHIR, HL7, SNOMED, ICD), contribute back to open-source communities, and share our work so the whole African health ecosystem benefits, not just our clients.

**Local roots, continental reach** — We are a Kenyan company that thinks continentally. We hire locally, build local capacity, and design for African realities — low-bandwidth environments, mobile-first users, multilingual communities — while keeping pan-African scalability in mind from day one.

**Drive to deliver** — We have a strong bias toward shipping real, working software over polished presentations. We keep commitments, iterate rapidly, and measure ourselves by the impact our software creates in clinical settings, not the elegance of our architecture diagrams.

**Learning and mentoring** — We are passionate about continuous growth. Whether it is improving our FHIR implementation, deepening our understanding of Kenyan health policy, or coaching a junior engineer, we invest in knowledge and share it freely.

**One team** — Clinicians, engineers, health informatics specialists, and delivery managers work as a single integrated team. We do not hand off — we collaborate, and we hold each other accountable for outcomes, not just outputs.
